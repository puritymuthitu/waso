The department comprises two subjects, namely;

  • English - Literature and English
  • Kiswahili - Fasihi and Lugha

There are seven languages teachers recruited by the Teachers Services Commission, with four teaching English and three Kiswahili.
The department came up with school language policy whereby there days set for students to speak in English or Kiswahili. Monday, Tuesday and Thursday students only communicate in English whereas, Wednesday and Friday students communicate in Kiswahili.
