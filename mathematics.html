The department has five able mathematics teachers recruited by the Teachers Service Commission. The members embrace teamwork, discipline, strict and consistent testing of candidates. Over the years, there has been progressive improvement in performance, which is attributed to the hard work, dedication, personal commitment and collective responsibility of the members.

Every Friday after the day's lessons, senior classes (form 3 and form 4), do a speed text, which is marked over the weekend and the results are released on Monday during the morning assembly with the best and most improved students being recognized and rewarded.
