The department comprises four subjects, namely;

  • Business Studies
  • Computer Studies
  • Agriculture
  • Home Science

There are six teachers in the technical department recruited by the Teachers Services Commission, with two teaching Business Studies, two Computer Studies, one Agriculture and one Home Science.

In junior classes Business Studies and Computer Studies are compulsory, whereas one has to either do Agriculture or Home Science. In senior classes one can only choose to do one or two techinical subjects depending on their future career.
